How to add image in web page.

We can use this attribute:-> src, height, width, alt, align, border, title, vspace(Vertical space), hspace(Horizontal space) etc.

Height--It is used for reduce and increase height.

Width---It is used for reduce and increase width.

Border--It is used for reduce and increase border size of outside of picture.

Title---It is used for when you keep the cursor on this image then you can see the title.

Align---It is used for location setup of image:-> Left, Right, Top and Botom.

Hspace--It is used for Horizontaly(Nese=nese) space count.

Vspace--It is used for Verticaly(Opor=Nise) Space count.
